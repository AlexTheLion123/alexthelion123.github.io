COURSES

END USER COMPUTING

NQF LEVEL 3

This qualification is designed to meet the needs of learners who require End User Computing skills in all sectors of the economy. Career opportunities include Call Centre Administrator, Application Support, Project Administrator or Helpdesk Officer.

Career Opportunities
  • Call centre administrator
  • Application support
  • Project administrator
  • Help-desk officer
Curriculum Cores
  • Microsoft Office Suit (basic, intermediate and advanced)
  • Word
  • Excel
  • PowerPoint
  • Access – Email (Electronic Mail Application)
  • Internet (WebBrowser Application)
What’s included
  • Online learning environment
  • Facilitator
  • Assessment and Portfolio based
  • Follows MICT Seta and QCTO standards (Accredited course)
Duration: 1 Year
Requirements: Grade 9 – 12

SYSTEM DEVELOPMENT

NQF LEVEL 4

This qualification is designed to build a foundational entry into the field of Computer Sciences and Information Technology, specifically into the field of Systems Development. The course covers the basic knowledge needed for further study in the field of Systems Development at Higher Education Levels.

Career Opportunities
  • System analyst
  • Front-end developer
  • Information systems security
  • Intermediate iOS developer
Curriculum Cores
  • Demonstrate an understanding of handling of error in a computer programming environment
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the principles of the internet and the world-wide web
What’s included
  • Online learning environment
  • Assessment and Portfolio based
  • Follows MICT Seta and QCTO standards (Accredited course)
Duration: 1 Year
Requirements: Grade 10 – 12
