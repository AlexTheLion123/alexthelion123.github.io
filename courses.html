COURSES

END USER COMPUTING

NQF LEVEL 3

This qualification is designed to meet the needs of learners who require End User Computing skills in all sectors of the economy. Career opportunities include Call Centre Administrator, Application Support, Project Administrator or Helpdesk Officer.

Curriculum Cores
  • Microsoft Office Suit (basic, intermediate and advanced)
  • Word
  • Excel
  • PowerPoint
  • Access – Email (Electronic Mail Application)
  • Internet (WebBrowser Application)
What’s included
  • Online learning environment
  • Facilitator
  • Assessment and Portfolio based
  • Follows MICT Seta and QCTO standards (Accredited course)
Duration: 1 Year
Requirements: Grade 9 – 12
